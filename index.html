Welcome, I'm Melanie.

My home is located in Miami, Florida.

Background

I'm a professional seamstress, striving to share my ideas and designs with the world by opening my own business. I used to hem and alter clothing for my family when I was younger, and that is when I realized this was more than just a hobby.

Sewing gave me an outlet for creativity and a fulfilling job. It also provided me with a sense of peace when I was struggling with overwhelming stress.

Services

Over the years, I have been able to expand my skillset and will be able to provide the following services:

  • Alterations
  • Sizing
  • Designing
  • Custom-clothing

I aspire to make dreams come true with a little fabric and thread, everybody deserves their Cinderella moment.
